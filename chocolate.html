CHOCOLATE RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 ½ cups unsweetened cocoa powder, plus more for dusting
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda
  • ¾ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 3 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 ½ cups hot water
